How to Play

1. Choose your Sawyer character and enter your name.

2. Use the mouse to move your player and try to score.

3. The game ends when you reach 10 goals.

4. Check your score in the game over screen!

5. Top scores only store locally on your browser. Working to fix this in future versions.
